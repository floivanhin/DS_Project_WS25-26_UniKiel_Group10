RQ9: How is team age related to shot efficiency?

For all 18 Bundesliga teams in the 2024/25 season, average team age was linked with `goals_per_shot`. The data shows a negative relationship: older teams tend to be less efficient in finishing.

Answer to RQ9

At team level, there is a negative association between average age and shot efficiency. Younger to mid-age squads tend to show higher `goals_per_shot` values.

The quadratic model does not provide a stable single optimum age within the observed range.

Key findings

  • Top efficiency: Bayern Munich (0.153), Borussia Dortmund (0.147), Bayer Leverkusen (0.143).
  • Low efficiency: St. Pauli (0.074), VfL Bochum (0.076), 1. FC Union Berlin (0.084).
  • Model note: maximum lies outside the observed range of 24.25-27.86.
