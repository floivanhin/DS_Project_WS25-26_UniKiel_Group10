What does our Bundesliga analysis investigate?

We analyze the 2024/25 Bundesliga season with a focus on player age, team structure, and performance metrics. The next pages answer RQ4 (home-away effect in player ratings) and RQ9 (age vs. shot efficiency).

Project and methodology

The pipeline loads team and match data, merges it into a consistent tabular format, and stores outputs in `data`. This makes the RQ4 and RQ9 metrics reproducible.

  • `RQ4`: home-vs-away analysis using aggregated WhoScored player ratings.
  • `RQ9`: relationship between average team age and shot efficiency (`goals_per_shot`).
  • Additional player age-band analysis for efficiency interpretation.

What is already answered?

The menu pages RQ4 and RQ9 already include concrete results from your CSV outputs with interactive charts.

  • RQ4 shows a small overall home advantage in player ratings.
  • RQ9 shows a negative relationship between team age and shot efficiency.
  • The best player-level age band in this dataset is 33 years.
