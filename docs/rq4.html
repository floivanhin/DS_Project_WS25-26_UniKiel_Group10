Answer to RQ4

On average, home ratings are slightly higher than away ratings. This indicates a small overall home effect in player ratings.

At the same time, the effect is not identical for all players: several players show strong positive or negative deltas.

Key findings

  • Strongest positive deltas: Lucas Hoeler (+0.785), Nico Schlotterbeck (+0.773), Felix Nmecha (+0.757).
  • Strongest negative deltas: Timo Horn (-0.735), Jakov Medic (-0.591), Nathan Ngoumou (-0.588).
  • The top home and away average rating is Omar Marmoush.
