Research Question

RQ9: How does the average player age affect a team's efficiency (goals per shot)?

This page answers the research question using reproducible Bundesliga 2024/25 analysis outputs and highlights the key age-vs-efficiency findings.

Answer to RQ9

The overall relationship remains negative: younger squads tend to post better shot efficiency in this Bundesliga sample.

The fitted quadratic peak still lies outside the observed team-age range, so the data supports a trend, not one exact optimal team-average age.

  • The best age band can move when you raise the minimum shot threshold.
